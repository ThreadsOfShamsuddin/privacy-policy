Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our internal inventory-management and marketing application ("the Application") handles data in relation to our Etsy storefront operations.

1. Information We Collect and Access

Our application accesses public product data directly from our storefront catalog, including listing titles, text descriptions, and product image URLs.

2. How We Use the Data

This data is used exclusively to format, crop, and optimize product marketing assets for publication to the Pinterest API.

3. Third-Party Disclosures (AI Processing)

To optimize content descriptions and select the highest-performing visual assets, public product image URLs and text titles are passed securely to third-party artificial intelligence text and vision processing APIs (specifically OpenAI). No personally identifiable customer data, order history, or financial metrics are ever shared with third-party AI services.

4. Data Retention and Security

Our application does not harvest, store, track, or sell any personal Pinterest user data. Pinterest API authentication keys and refresh tokens are stored strictly locally and securely within our private server infrastructure.
